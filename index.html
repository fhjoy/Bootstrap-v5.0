Who Am I

I Md Faisal Hossain, student, recently I have completed my third semester as an international postgraduate student in Communication and Media Engineering at Hochschule Offenburg and I did my bachelor's in Computer Science and Engineering. Punctuality, honesty, and independence are the three most important factors for me. By working in a multinational company, I have enhanced my communication skills, and it also helped me to develop organizational skills like multitasking and being an active team player. In addition to my knowledge, I always actively seek out new technologies and stay up-to-date on industry trends and advancements. I know this will allow me to stay ahead of the curve and deliver exceptional work to all of my employers. I also want to say that I think am a fast learner, can also participate in a new project in an independent/team, efficient, and solution-orientated manner.
